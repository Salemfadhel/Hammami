About Charlie Coin

Charlie Coin (CHR) is a cryptocurrency created in memory of Charlie Chaplin, one of the greatest silent film stars of all time. The aim of this digital currency is to honor Chaplin's enduring legacy and bring a sense of creativity and humor to the blockchain world.
